Our Enchanted Story

A Hidden Paradise

Nestled behind an unassuming ivy-covered gate lies The Secret Garden Café, a magical retreat discovered by our founders, Evelyn and Theodore Greenleaf, during their travels through Europe in 2010.

What began as an abandoned Victorian greenhouse has blossomed into a sanctuary where nature and gastronomy intertwine.

Our Philosophy

We believe in the magic of fresh, seasonal ingredients sourced from our own garden and local organic farms. Every dish tells a story, every cocktail casts a spell, and every visit becomes a cherished memory.
